About JOOUST Dialogue Works: Empowering Young Leaders.

About

"Dialogue Works Club is a youth club dedicated to fostering leadership skills and empowering young people in Siaya, Kenya. We believe that young people have a powerful voice and deserve to be heard."

Become effective leaders: We collaborate with other organizations to provide workshops and training sessions that develop leadership skills like communication, collaboration, and problem-solving.

Understand their rights: We educate young people about their rights and responsibilities, empowering them to advocate for themselves and their communities.

Amplify their voices: We offer a platform for young people to express their ideas and concerns, fostering dialogue and positive change. Dialogue Works Club is a space where young people can connect, learn, and grow.

We believe in the power of youth to make a difference in the world. Join us and be part of the change!!!

Our Vision

A society where young people actively contribute to positive change and their ideas, rights and voices are fully respected.

Our Mission

Dialogue Works Club mission is to address the challenges and issues that affect young people and to promote social and environmental stability by empowering the young people with knowledge on their rights and leadership skills.

Our Purpose

To help youth prepare for their future roles, develop their talents and interests, learn leadership skills, and foster friendships and networks as they interact with other individuals across the regions.

President of Dialogue Works

Meet Yare Phenyle, the visionary leader of Dialogue Works Club. With a strong commitment to empowering youth and promoting leadership skills, Yare Phenyle has been instrumental in shaping the mission of Dialogue Works. Under her leadership, the organization has expanded its reach and impact, helping young people find their voice and make a difference in the communities.
